Ishmael

Facts:

Ishmael was the son of Abraham and Sarah's servant, Hagar.

  • Ishmael was not the son through whom God would fulfill his promise to Abraham.
  • Ishmael means, “God hears.”

[See also: Abraham, Sarah]

Examples from the Bible stories:

  • [05-02] So Abram married Hagar. Hagar had a baby boy, and Abram named him Ishmael.
  • [05-04] “I will make Ishmael a great nation, too, but my covenant will be with Isaac.”
