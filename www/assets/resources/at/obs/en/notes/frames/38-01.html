The Passover [38-01]

Every year, the Jews celebrated the Passover. This was a celebration of how God had saved their ancestors from slavery in Egyptmany centuries earlier. About three years after Jesus first began preaching and teaching publicly, Jesus told his disciples that he wanted to celebrate this Passover with them in Jerusalem, and that he would be killed there.

Translation Notes:

  • many centuries earlier - That is, “hundreds of years earlier” or, “a very long time before.”
