Building the Boat [03-04]

NoahobeyedGod. He and his three sons built the boat just the way God had told them. It took many years to build the boat, because it was so big. Noah warned the people about the flood that was coming and told them to turn to God, but they did not believe him.

Translation Notes:

  • Noah warned - Noah told everyone that God planned to destroy the world because of sin.
  • turn to God - This means that they should stop sinning and start obeying God.
