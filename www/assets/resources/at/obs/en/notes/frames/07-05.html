Esau Hated Jacob [07-05]

Esau hated Jacob because Jacob had stolen his rights as oldest son and also his blessing. So he planned to kill Jacob after their father died.

Translation Notes:

  • rights as oldest son - Jacob found a way to get the larger portion of their father's wealth that should have gone to Esau as the older son. See also note on [07-02].
  • his blessing - Jacob also tricked their father into giving him the promise of extra prosperity that Isaac intended to give to Esau. See also note on [07-03].
