A Storm Ended the Drought [19-13]

Then Elijah said to KingAhab, “Return immediately to the city, because the rain is coming.” Soon the sky grew black, and a heavy rain began. Yahweh had ended the drought and proved that he is the true God.

Translation Notes:

  • the sky grew black - That is, “the sky became very dark.” Heavy rainclouds covered the sky, making it look dark grey or black.
  • drought – That is, “long dry time with no rain.”
