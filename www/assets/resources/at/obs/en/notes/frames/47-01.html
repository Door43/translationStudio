Paul and Silas in Philippi [47-01]

As Saul traveled throughout the Roman Empire, he began to use his Roman name, ”Paul.” One day, Paul and his friend Silas went to the town of Philippi to proclaim the good news about Jesus. They went to a place by the river outside the city where people gathered to pray. There they met a woman named Lydia who was a merchant. She loved and worshipedGod.

Translation Notes:

  • proclaim - That is, “teach” or, “preach.”
  • merchant - This could also be translated as, “seller” or, “business owner.”
