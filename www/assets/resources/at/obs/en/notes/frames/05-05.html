Birth of Isaac [05-05]

That day Abrahamcircumcisedall the males in his household. About a year later, when Abraham was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham's son. They named him Isaac as God had told them to do.

Translation Notes:

  • all the males in his household - This means all of the men and boys that Abraham was responsible for, including his male servants, young and old.
