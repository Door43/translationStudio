The Shepherds Visited Jesus [23-08]

The shepherds soon arrived at the place where Jesus was and they found him lying in a feeding trough, just as the angel had told them. They were very excited. Mary was very happy, too. The shepherds returned to the fields where their sheep were, praisingGod for all they had heard and seen.

Translation Notes:

  • all they had heard and seen - That is, “for everything they had heard and seen.” This included the glorious angels and their amazing message, as well as seeing the newborn Messiah himself.
