Persecution and Hardship [33-07]

“The rocky groundis a person who hears God’s word and accepts it with joy. But when he experiences hardship or persecution, he falls away.”

Translation Notes:

  • (Jesus continues to explain the story.)
  • rocky ground - See how you translated this term in [33-03].
  • is a person - See how you translated this comparison in [33-06].
  • accepts it with joy - That is, “believes it joyfully” or, “joyfully agrees that it is true.”
  • falls away - That is, “no longer follows God or obeys him” or, “stops following or obeying God.”
