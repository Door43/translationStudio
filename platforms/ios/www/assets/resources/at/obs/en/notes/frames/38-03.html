Thirty Pieces of Silver [38-03]

The Jewish leaders, led by the high priest, paid Judasthirty silver coins to betrayJesus. This happened just as the prophets had predicted. Judas agreed, took the money, and went away. He began looking for an opportunity to help them arrest Jesus.

Translation Notes:

  • thirty silver coins - Each of these coins were worth about four days' wages.
