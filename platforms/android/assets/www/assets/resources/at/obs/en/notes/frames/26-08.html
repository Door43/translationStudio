Jesus Healed the Sick [26-08]

Then Jesuswent throughout the region of Galilee, and large crowds came to him. They brought many people who were sick or handicapped, including those who could not see, walk, hear, or speak, and Jesus healed them.

Translation Notes:

  • went throughout the region of Galilee - That is, “all around in Galilee” or, “from place to place in Galilee.”
