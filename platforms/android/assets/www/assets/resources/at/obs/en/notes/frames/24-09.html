Jesus Is the Son of God [24-09]

God had told John, “The Holy Spiritwill come down and rest on someone you baptize. That person is the Son of God.” There is only one God. But John heard God the Father speak, and saw Jesus the Son and the Holy Spirit when he baptized Jesus.

A Bible story from: Matthew 3; Mark 1:9-11; Luke 3:1-23

Translation Notes:

  • God had told John - That is, “Before this, God had told John” or, “Before Jesus came to be baptized, God had told John.”
  • A Bible story from - These references may be slightly different in some Bible translations.
