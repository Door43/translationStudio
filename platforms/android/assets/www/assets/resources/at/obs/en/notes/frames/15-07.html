The Gibeonites in Danger [15-07]

The Israelites were angry when they found out that the Gibeonites had deceived them, but they kept the peace treaty they had made with them because it was a promise before God. Some time later, the kings of another people group in Canaan, the Amorites, heard that the Gibeonites had made a peace treaty with the Israelites, so they combined their armies into one large army and attacked Gibeon. The Gibeonites sent a message to Joshua asking for help.

Translation Notes:

  • sent a message to Joshua asking for help - This could be translated as, “sent some of their people to tell Joshua that they needed the Israelites to help defend them against their enemies.”
