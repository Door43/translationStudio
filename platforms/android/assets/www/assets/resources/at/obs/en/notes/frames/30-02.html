The Crowd Followed Jesus [30-02]

But there were many people who saw Jesus and the disciplesleave in the boat. These people ran along the shore of the lake to get to the other side ahead of them. So when Jesus and the disciples arrived, a large group of people was already there, waiting for them.

Translation Notes:

  • leave in the boat - Some languages may need to add, “to cross the lake.”
  • arrived - That is, “got to the other side of the lake” or, “got there.”
