To Prison [29-06]

“The fellow servantfell on his knees and said, ‘Please be patient with me, and I will pay the full amount that I owe you.’ But instead, the servant threw his fellow servant into prison until he could pay the debt.”

Translation Notes:

  • (Jesus continues the story.)
  • fellow servant - Translate this phrase in the same way as in [29-05].
  • fell on his knees - This phrase has the same meaning as in [29-04].
  • threw his fellow servant into prison - This could also be translated as, “had the man put into prison.” The word “threw” is figurative and means it was done in a forceful way.
