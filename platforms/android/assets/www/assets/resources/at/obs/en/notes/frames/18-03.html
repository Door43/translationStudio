Solomon Served Other Gods [18-03]

But Solomon loved women from other countries. He disobeyedGod by marrying many women, almost 1,000 of them! Many of these women came from foreign countries and brought their gods with them and continued to worship them. When Solomon was old, he also worshiped their gods.

Translation Notes:

  • brought their gods with them - They brought their idols and their methods of worshiping idols with them to Israel.
