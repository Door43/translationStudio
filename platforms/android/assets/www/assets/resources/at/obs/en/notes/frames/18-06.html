Rehoboam's Foolish Answer [18-06]

Rehoboamfoolishly answered them, “You thought my father Solomon made you work hard, but I will make you work harder than he did, and I will punish you more harshly than he did.”

Translation Notes:

  • foolishly answered - Rehoboam's answer was harsh, and caused the people to turn against him.
