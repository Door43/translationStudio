Babylon Destroyed Judah [20-07]

But after a few years, the king of Judahrebelled against Babylon. So, the Babylonians came back and attacked the kingdom of Judah. They captured the city of Jerusalem, destroyed the Temple, and took away all the treasures of the city and the Temple.

Translation Notes:

  • came back - This may also be translated as, “returned” or “came to Judah again.”
  • captured the city - That is, “took control of the city and the people there.”
  • took away - They carried the treasures with them back to Babylon.
