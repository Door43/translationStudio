David Wanted to Build a Temple [17-06]

David wanted to build a temple where all the Israelites could worshipGod and offer him sacrifices. For about 400 years, the people had been worshiping God and offering sacrifices to him at the Tent of Meeting that Moses had built.

Translation Notes:

  • build a temple - David wanted to construct a permanent building for worshiping God that would replace the portable Tent of Meeting.
