God's People [48-07]

God told Abraham, “All the people groups of the earth will be blessed through you.” Jesus was a descendantof Abraham. All the people groups are blessed through him, because everyone who believes in Jesus is saved from sin, and becomes a spiritual descendant of Abraham.

Translation Notes:

  • all the people groups of the earth – This could also be translated as, “people from all different parts of the world.”
  • a spiritual descendant of Abraham - That is, “spiritually related to Abraham, and one of God's people.”
