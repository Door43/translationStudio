The Wicked Servant [29-08]

“The kingcalled the servant and said, ‘You wicked servant! I forgave your debt because you begged me. You should have done the same.’ The king was so angry that he threw the wicked servant into prison until he could pay back all of his debt.”

Translation Notes:

  • (Jesus continues the story.)
  • called the servant - That is, “commanded the servant to come to him” or, “ordered his guards to bring the servant to him.”
  • begged me – This could be translated as, “pleaded with me” or, “urgently asked me to be merciful.”
  • You should have done the same - That is, “You should have forgiven the man who owed you, just as I forgave you.”
  • threw - That is, “commanded his guards to put.” Consider how “threw” was translated in frame [29-06].
