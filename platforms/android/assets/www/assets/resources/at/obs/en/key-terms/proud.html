proud

Definition:

A proud person thinks of himself as better than others, or as more important than others, and often does not admit his faults.

  • Other English words that mean the same as proud are “arrogant,” “conceited,” and “self-important”.
  • “They were proud” could be translated as “They thought highly of themselves.”

Being proud can lead a person to disobey God.

The opposite of “proud” is “humble.”

[See also: humble]

Examples from the Bible stories:

  • [04-02] They were very proud, and they did not care about what God said.
  • [34-10] Then Jesus said, “I tell you the truth, God heard the tax collector’s prayer and declared him to be righteous. But he did not like the prayer of the religious leader. God will humble everyone who is proud, and he will lift up whoever humbles himself.”
