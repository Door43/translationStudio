Joseph in Egypt [08-04]

The slave traders took Joseph to Egypt. Egypt was a large, powerful country located along the Nile River. The slave traders sold Joseph as a slave to a wealthy government official. Joseph served his master well, and Godblessed Joseph.

Translation Notes:

  • government official - This was a person who was part of the Egyptian government. Another way to say this would be, “a leader in Egypt's government.”
