God Hates Sin [49-08]

Jesus also told us that God hates sin. When Adam and Evesinned, it affected all of their descendants. As a result, every person in the world sins and is separated from God. Therefore, everyone has become an enemy of God.

Translation Notes:

  • is separated from God - This can also be translated as, “cannot live with God” or, “cannot be near God” or, “cannot have a relationship with God.”
