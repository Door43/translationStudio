Peter and John Were Arrested [44-06]

The leaders of the Temple were very upset by what Peter and John were saying. So they arrested them and put them into prison. But many of the people believedPeter's message, and the number of men who believed in Jesus grew to about 5,000.

Translation Notes:

  • Peter's message - That is, “the message that Peter had preached to them.”
  • number of men - This was in addition to the woman and children who believed.
