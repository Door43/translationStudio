The Large Tree [34-02]

“But when the mustard seedgrows, it becomes the largest of all of the garden plants, big enough that even the birds come and rest in its branches.”

Translation Notes:

  • (Jesus continues the story.)
  • mustard seed - See how you translated this term in [34-01].
  • grows – You may want to add, “into a mature plant.”
  • garden plants – This can also be translated as, “plants that would be planted in a garden.”
  • rest in - This may also be translated as, “sit on” or, “perch on.”
